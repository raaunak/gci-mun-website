Travel & Flight Guidance

Delegates travelling internationally should plan arrival at Tribhuvan International Airport (KTM). We recommend booking flights early for best rates. Arrival assistance can be provided on request.

Accommodation Options

  • Hotel Yak & Yeti: Premium option near conference venue
  • Kathmandu Guest House: Mid-range with cultural charm
  • Hotel Shanker: Historic hotel with excellent service

Optional discounted rates may be available for international delegations. Contact gcimun@gci.edu.np for group bookings.

Pricing & Assistance

International delegates may be eligible for travel grants or sponsorship letters upon request. Provide your travel itinerary early to secure stock letters.

Residential Delegation Registration

Includes Accommodation + Meals

Residential delegates enjoy on-campus accommodation, meals during conference days, and access to all sessions.

Register Residential Delegation

Accommodation & Meals

Accommodation is provided in safe and comfortable dorms near the conference venue. Meal plans include breakfast, lunch, and dinner for the duration of your stay.

Local Travel

Residential delegates receive complimentary local transport to/from the venue. Shuttle services operate each day before sessions begin.

Pricing Details

Pricing includes accommodation for all conference nights + three meals daily + conference materials. Group discounts available upon request.

Support & Contacts

For assistance with booking, travel questions, or itinerary support, email gcimun@gci.edu.np.

Payment Details

All payments should be made via bank transfer to the official GCI MUN account. Please email your payment confirmation and transaction ID to gcimun@gci.edu.np.

Payment deadlines will be updated closer to the conference. Late payments may be subject to additional fees.
