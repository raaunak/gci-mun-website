What is GCI MUN?

GCIMUN, the Global College International Model United Nations, is one of Nepal’s longstanding and influential MUN conferences. Its history dates back to 2018, when it began as UGCIMUN before evolving into a fully independent conference organized exclusively by the A-Level faculty of Global College International.

With this year marking its seventh iteration, GCIMUN has established itself as a consistent platform for academic engagement, global awareness, and youth leadership.

Why GCI MUN?

Experienced Leadership/ Board members with International Exposure

With international board members contributing to committee design and evaluation, the conference reflects global MUN standards rather than purely local practices.

Global Awareness

Students explore real-world issues and gain a deeper understanding of international affairs.

Community

GCI MUN fosters a welcoming community where participants build lifelong friendships.
